# Contributor Covenant Code of Conduct

## Our Pledge

We as members, contributors, and maintainers pledge to make participation in LiteClaw a harassment-free experience for everyone.

## Our Standards

Examples of behavior that contributes to a positive environment:

- Giving and gracefully accepting constructive feedback
- Focusing on technical clarity and reproducibility
- Respecting differing viewpoints and experience levels
- Taking responsibility for mistakes and fixing impact quickly

Examples of unacceptable behavior:

- Harassment, intimidation, or discriminatory language
- Personal attacks, trolling, or inflammatory behavior
- Publishing private information without permission
- Deliberate disruption of project discussion or review

## Enforcement Responsibilities

Maintainers are responsible for clarifying and enforcing this Code of Conduct.

## Scope

This Code of Conduct applies in all project spaces and public spaces when someone is representing LiteClaw.

## Enforcement

Report incidents to: `conduct@liteclaw.dev` (placeholder; update before public launch).

All reports will be reviewed and investigated promptly and fairly.
